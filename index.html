Ashley Song

Environmental Studies

Hello, my name is Ashley Song. I am a Dedicated Environmental Studies major with experience in animal care, hands on experience engaging with coastal environments, and a passion for managing customer relations. I am deeply Committed to environmental responsibility and community involvement.

I’m originally from Irvine, California and graduated from Northwood High school in 2025. I am now a first year at the University of California of Riverside where I am pursuing a degree in environmental studies. My passion for the environment stems from my love of nature and the outdoors in general. I am especially interested in invasive species ecology and how these species affect local ecosystems. My interests have led me to volunteer at the OC animal care where I care for and adopt out stray kittens along with educating the public about sustainable and responsible cat ownership.

As I continue to build my professional experience, I aspire to get involved with clubs along with research opportunities on campus. As I complete more of these goals I will update this page accordingly. Outside of academics, some of my hobbies include things like digital art, sewing, sculpting, and costume making. Most of my art is made up of upcycled materials. As I mentioned above I also love to care for animals. I volunteer multiple times a week at my local animal shelter. I also train my own cat to do fun little tricks as an enrichment activity.
